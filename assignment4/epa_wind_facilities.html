üìã Technical Notes & Definitions

  • Nameplate Capacity: Maximum rated electrical output under optimal wind conditions, expressed in megawatts (MW)
  • COD (Commercial Operation Date): Date when facility achieved substantial completion and began delivering power to the grid
  • Configuration: General project type; specific turbine models and manufacturers available upon request
  • Coordinates: Geographic centroid of turbine array in WGS84 datum (EPSG:4326)
  • Hub Height: Varies by project; typical range 80-140 meters above ground level

Data Sources: FERC Form 556 (Electric Quarterly Reports), State Public Utility Commission filings, EPA Renewable Energy Tracking System (RETS), and direct operator submissions. Database maintained by EPA Office of Atmospheric Programs, Clean Energy Division.
