üìñ Data Field Definitions

Project Name
Official facility designation as registered with state PUC
Technology
PV = Photovoltaic panels; CSP = Concentrated Solar Power
Capacity
DC nameplate rating in megawatts (MW)
GPS Location
Approximate facility centroid in WGS84 decimal degrees
Year Online
Year of commercial operation or substantial completion
Data Quality Notes:
  • Capacity figures represent DC nameplate rating and may differ from AC output capacity
  • GPS coordinates are approximate facility centroids; large solar farms may span multiple parcels
  • Operational dates reflect commercial operation date (COD) as reported to state regulators
  • Technology classifications follow EPA Renewable Energy Certificate (REC) standards
